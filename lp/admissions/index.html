GECET (Graphic Era Common Entrance Test) is our primary entrance test for various UG and PG programs. While it is mandatory for specific technical courses, it also serves as the gateway to securing merit-based scholarships worth up to 100% of the tuition fee.
Yes, GEHU provides a special 25% fee concession on tuition fees for students who are bona fide residents of Uttarakhand. This is in addition to merit-based scholarships earned through GECET or qualifying board exam percentages.
